Contextual Transformer AES

University of Edinburgh

April 2023 - August 2023

• Analysed the role of contextual information in Automated Essay Scoring using 12,876 essays from the ASAP-AES dataset.

• Evaluated context sensitivity across RoBERTa, DeBERTaV3, and Longformer models, using Quadratic Weighted Kappa (QWK) and Mean Squared Error (MSE).

• Achieved an average QWK of 0.823 across all prompts; reached 0.8697 QWK in intra-set training, outperforming SoTA transformer models.
