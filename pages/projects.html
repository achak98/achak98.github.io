Contextual Transformer AES

University of Edinburgh (a.k.a. "the dissertation")

April 2023 - August 2023

• Analysed the role of contextual information in Automated Essay Scoring using 12,876 essays from the ASAP-AES dataset across RoBERTa, DeBERTaV3, and Longformer.

• Achieved 0.823 average QWK across all prompts, peaking at 0.8697 in intra-set training – outperforming state-of-the-art transformer models.

• Demonstrated that context contributes substantially to scoring performance, independent of model architecture. Context really does matter – in essays and in life.
